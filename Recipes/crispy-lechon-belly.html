Chrispy Lechon Belly

Description

Crispy Lechon Belly has so many names. It is called bellychon, lechon belly, lechon liempo, roasted pork belly, and what have you. One thing is for sure, whatever this dish is referred to, the taste and flavor should be the same. This means that it should be tasty and delicious while being crispy on the outside.

Ingredients

  • 6 lbs. whole slab boneless pork belly
  • 1 large bunch lemongrass pounded
  • 1 large bunch scallions

    • Rub Ingredients

  • 1 tablespoon salt
  • 1 1/2 tablespoon garlic powder
  • 1 teaspoon ground black pepper

    • Steaming Ingredients

  • 7 pieces star anise
  • 1 medium red onion wedged
  • 1 pint water

Instructions

  1. Combine rub ingredients in a bowl. Mix well.Rub all over the pork belly.
  2. Arrange scallions and pounded lemongrass in the middle of the slab. Roll. Use a kitchen twine to secure the rolled pork belly. Set aside.
  3. Preheat oven to 400F
  4. Add star anise and wedged onion into the roaster. Pour 1 pint water.
  5. Arrange rolled pork belly over the roasting pan. Securely wrap the roasting pan with an aluminum foil.
  6. Bake for 2 hours.
  7. Remove the foil. Increase the heat to 475F. Roast for 1 1/2 hours.
  8. Remove from the oven. Let the meat cool down.
  9. Slice into serving pieces. Serve.
  10. Share and enjoy!
